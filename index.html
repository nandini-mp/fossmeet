IMPORTANCE OF FOSS

Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) is vital to modern technology, promoting innovation and accessibility by making source code freely available for modification and distribution. It democratizes access to tools and platforms, enabling faster development of diverse applications. FOSS fosters global collaboration, allowing developers to work together, share expertise, and improve software quality. By encouraging transparency and flexibility, FOSS ensures software remains secure and adaptable, driving progress in technology through open collaboration and inclusivity.
